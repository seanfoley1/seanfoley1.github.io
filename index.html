Welcome to Sean Foley Equine Physical Therapy

Qualified and Insured Equine Physical Therapist based in West Wicklow

Our Services

  • Evaluating the horse's physical condition and identifying any areas of concern.
  • Creating personalized treatment plans to address specific issues and promote healing.
  • Performing hands-on techniques such as massages, stretches, and exercises to improve the horse's range of motion and flexibility.
  • Collaborating with veterinarians, trainers, and other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care for the horse.
  • Educating the horse's owners or caregivers on proper techniques for maintaining the horse's physical health and preventing future injuries.
