Welcome to Sean Foley Equine and Canine Physical Therapy

Qualified and Insured Equine and Canine Physical Therapist based in West Wicklow

Our Services

  • Evaluate the animal's physical condition and identify any areas of concern.
  • Evaluate range of motion and muscular condition, including a dynamic assessment to analyse movement.
  • Perform hands on techniques such as massage, stretching and releases to improve range of motion and flexibility.
  • Identify muscular issues using palpation techniques and working to eliminate tension, soreness and restriction.
  • Collaborating with veterinarians and other healthcare professionals to insure comprehensive care.
  • Qualified and insured
