About Canine Physical Therapy

Dogs of any age and breed can benefit from therapy and massage. I assess movement, range of motion and muscular condition.

Using a range of hands on techniques, including massage and stretching and tailoring the treatment to meet the dogs specific needs.

  • Increase performance.
  • Relieve muscle tension and soreness.
  • Reduce muscle adhesion.
  • Relieve muscle fatigue and remove lactic acid buildup.
  • Restore/Improve range of motion.
  • Improve recovery and reduce the risk of future injury.
  • Improve muscle tone and condition.
